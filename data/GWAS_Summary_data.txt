The data that was used for the analysis was obtained from OpenGWAS (https://gwas.mrcieu.ac.uk/datasets/), a repository of GWAS summary statistics. The data is directly accessible through the TwoSampleMR package that requires the user to input the GWAS ID.
For this analysis, the OpenGWAS datasets had IDs met-d-Omega_3 and ebi-a-GCST010681
